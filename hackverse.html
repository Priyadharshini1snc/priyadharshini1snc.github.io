◈ About The Event

WHAT IS
HACKVERSE?

HackVerse is a 36-hour intercollegiate hackathon that brings together the brightest minds in tech. Whether you're a coder, designer, or problem-solver — if you've got the drive to build, this is your arena.

⚡
36-Hour Sprint
Non-stop hacking from kickoff to demo day. Fuel, food, and caffeine provided.
🧠
Expert Mentorship
50+ mentors from Google, Microsoft, Razorpay and top startups guiding your team.
🏆
Real Prizes
₹2,00,000+ prize pool plus internship offers, swag packs, and investor connections.
🌐
Open to All Colleges
Teams of 2–4 from any college across India. All domains and skill levels welcome.

Any student currently enrolled in a college or university can participate. Teams of 2–4 members. Individual participation is also allowed but teaming up is recommended. No restrictions on department or year of study.
HackVerse is completely free to participate. We provide meals, snacks, and accommodation for outstation participants during the 36-hour event at no cost.

Bring your laptop, charger, college ID, and your hustle. We provide extension boards, Wi-Fi, food, energy drinks, and a killer vibe. Hardware hackers can bring their own microcontrollers/sensors.
